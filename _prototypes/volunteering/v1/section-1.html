3Individual questions

3.20Thinking of the last 12 months, have you taken part in any volunteering for any groups, clubs or organisations?

Show guidance

This question is asking you to think about any voluntary activities you have done for a group, club or organisation over the last 12 months.

What to include

We are measuring formal volunteering. This is any activity for a club, group or organisation that involves spending time doing something of your own free will that benefits the community, the environment, or individuals other than relatives.

Include

  • Helping out with clubs, groups or organisations; for example at schools, religious groups or social clubs.
  • Doing social media for a community group.
  • Sport and exercise - any supporting activity done for a sports club or group for example refereeing or coaching
  • Taking part in activities funded by your employer to benefit someone other than yourself or the organisation you work for.
  • Voluntary activities that lead to a qualification or certificate; for example Duke of Edinburgh Award or St John’s Ambulance.
  • Volunteering outside England and Wales, for example going abroad to volunteer or raise money for a club, group or organisation.

Do not include:

  • Paid employment, including for charities, voluntary or third sector organisations.
  • Court ordered activities, for example community service.
  • Unpaid trainee work e.g. internships, apprenticeships, work placements.
  • Giving money or donating.
  • Giving help or care to a close family member or friend, for example child minding, taking someone to hospital, doing someone’s shopping.
  • Work, paid or unpaid, in a family business.
