Completing the individual questions

If you are unable to answer the individual questions now we recommend asking your fellow householders to complete their own section when they can or get them to provide you with the relevent information.

Alternatively if you are unable to do either of these then you can invite them to complete their own surveys via text message to a mobile phone.
