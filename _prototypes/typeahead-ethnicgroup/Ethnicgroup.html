15. What is your ethnic group?

What is your ethnic group?
Show further guidance

It is for you to choose your ethnic group. For example this could be your family or cultural background, or it could be how you would describe yourself.

Please select the answer you think is most appropriate.

If you don’t feel the options apply to you, select ‘Other ethnic group’. You will then be able to write in the ethnic group you prefer to identify as at the next question.
