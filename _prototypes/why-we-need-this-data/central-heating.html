Why this question is important

We ask a question about central heating to create statistics on home energy use. This data is used to inform policy development and monitoring, as well as planning for the delivering of services. This allows local and central government to understand levels of renewable heating, fuel poverty, deprivation and to reduce excess winter deaths.
