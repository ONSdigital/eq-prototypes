Why this question is important

The unpaid care question is asked to collect statistics on unpaid carers. These statistics are used by charity organisations, researchers, local and central government. Data captured will help inform provision of support services to unpaid carers and those who are being cared for in local authorities, make and improve policy for unpaid carers and assess the needs of unpaid carers at local and national level.
