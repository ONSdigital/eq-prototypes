To request a paper census form to be sent to you in the post, call our free language helpline on 0800 587 2021 to speak to someone in your language. You can also request a paper census form online.

You must fill in your census form in English. You can also answer in Welsh if you live in Wales.

Someone else can complete your census for you, or help you if you canâ€™t do it yourself.

Translation booklets

You can download or print a translation booklet to help you complete your census on paper. The booklet has a translation of each census question and more information about the census in your language.

You must write your answers on the paper census form, not in the translation booklet

If you live in Wales

You can use the translation booklet for England, PDF document download, 866KB, 13 pages. Question 17 is not translated in the booklet as this is only for people living in Wales.

Question 17 translation

Can you understand, speak, read or write Welsh?

  • Understand spoken Welsh
  • Speak Welsh
  • Read Welsh
  • Write Welsh
  • OR none of the above

If you need help

You can call our free language helpline on 0800 587 2021 to get further help in your language.
