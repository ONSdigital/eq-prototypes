  3. Completing your census on paper

To fill in your census on paper, call our free language helpline on 0800 587 2021 to request a paper census form to be sent to you in the post.

You must fill in your census form in English

Someone else can complete your census for you, or help you if you can't do it yourself.

Translation booklets

You can download or print a translation booklet to help you complete your census on paper. The booklet has a translation of each census question and more information about the census in your language.

If you live in Wales

If you live in Wales, the census questions are slightly different. Please call us on 0800 XXXX XXXX if you need help with the Wales questions in your language.

You must write your answers on the paper census form, not in the translation booklet

If you need help

You can call our free language helpline on 0800 587 2021 to get further help in your language.
