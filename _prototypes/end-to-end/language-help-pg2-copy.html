To start your census online, you will need your access code printed on the invitation letter we sent you. Your 16-character access code will be a combination of letters and numbers.

!Keep this code safe. You will need to enter it every time you access your census.

You must fill in your census form in English. You can also answer in Welsh if you live in Wales.

Someone else can complete your census for you, or help you if you canâ€™t do it yourself.

We do not recommend using online translation tools to translate the census questions. If the translations are wrong, your answers could be incorrect.
