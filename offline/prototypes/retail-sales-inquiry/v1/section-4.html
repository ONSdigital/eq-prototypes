2 Retail turnover

2.1 For the reporting period, what was the value of the business’s total retail turnover?

Round your figures to the nearest £. Even if your figures are zero, please still complete.

Include:

  • VAT
  • internet sales
  • Great Britain to customers abroad

Exclude:

  • revenue from mobile phone network commission and top-up
  • sales from catering facilities used by customers
  • lottery sales and commission from lottery sales
  • sales of car accessories and motor vehicles
  • NHS receipts
